# NordicFleet — Beta Feedback Form

Copy these questions into a Google Form / Typeform, or just reply inline.
Short, honest answers beat polished ones. ~5 minutes.

## About you

1. **What kind of skier or coach are you?** (e.g. masters racer, junior
   coach, wax tech, citizen racer)
2. **How many pairs of skis do you actively manage?**
3. **What do you use today to track skis and wax?** (memory, tape on the
   ski, a spreadsheet, another app, nothing…)
4. **Which did you test?** iOS / web / both.

## Your experience

5. **Where did you get stuck or confused?** Be specific — which screen,
   what you were trying to do.
6. **What felt genuinely useful?**
7. **What felt like busywork** — data entry you'd never actually keep up?
8. **Did anything look broken, slow, or error out?** (What, and where?)

## The decisions that matter

9. **Would you use this next week**, on your own, without us asking? Why or
   why not?
10. **Would this help you on race day** — actually pick a ski/wax — or is
    it just a record after the fact?
11. **What would make this worth paying for?** (Or: what would it have to
    do before you'd pay anything?)
12. **What should be removed or simplified?** If you could delete one
    feature, what is it?

## Coaches only (if you tried coaching / Wax Truck)

13. **Did linking an athlete and seeing their fleet work the way you
    expected?**
14. **Was the Wax Truck (head-to-head wax testing) useful, gimmicky, or
    confusing?** Would you run it on a real morning?
15. **Would you trust the messaging/advisory to reach your athletes** in
    time before a race?

## One last thing

16. **If you had to describe NordicFleet to a teammate in one sentence,
    what would you say?**
17. **Anything else** — gripes, ideas, "I wish it just…"
