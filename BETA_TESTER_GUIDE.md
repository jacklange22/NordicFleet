# NordicFleet — Beta Tester Guide

Thanks for testing NordicFleet. You're one of the first real people to use
it, which makes your honest reactions genuinely valuable. This should take
**20–30 minutes**. There are no wrong answers — if something confuses you,
that's a bug in the app, not in you. Write it down exactly as it happened.

## What NordicFleet is

A logbook for your skis and wax. The idea: keep your whole quiver, every
wax job, and every on-snow test in one place, so on race morning you can
look back and pick the ski and wax that actually worked — instead of
guessing from memory.

## Before you start

- **Web:** open **https://app.nordicfleet.com** (or the link we sent) in a
  browser. Works on a laptop or phone browser.
- **iOS:** if we sent you a TestFlight invite, install from there. If you
  got a direct build, follow the install note we included.
- Use a **real** but throwaway-able email you can check (for password
  reset).

You don't need both platforms. Use whatever we asked you to.

## Your mission (the core loop)

Do these in order. Go at a normal pace — we want to see where it slows you
down.

1. **Create an account.** Sign up with email + password. If asked "do you
   coach a team?", answer honestly.
2. **Add at least 5 skis.** Use your real quiver if you can — brand,
   model, grind, length, flex, whatever you know. On web you can also try
   **Import** (paste from a spreadsheet) if you keep one.
3. **Log one wax.** Pick a ski, record what you'd actually put on it
   (binder/kick/glide). If a wax you use isn't in the suggestions, **just
   type it** — it should let you.
4. **Log one test.** Record conditions (snow, temp) and rate how a ski
   felt (glide/kick/etc.).
5. **Make a race-day decision.** Pretend you race tomorrow. Using only
   what's in the app, decide which ski + wax you'd choose. Note whether the
   app actually helped you decide, or whether you still had to guess.

### If you're a coach, also try:

6. **Turn on Coaching mode** (Profile → "Coach a team", or the mode
   switcher). Switch between My Fleet and Coaching.
7. **Link an athlete** (or have an athlete add you by your email) and look
   at their fleet.
8. **Send them a message / race-day advisory.**
9. **Try the Wax Truck** (the third mode). Create a test, add a few wax
   combinations (type in whatever you actually compare), generate the
   bracket, and run it head-to-head to a winner.

## When you're done

- **Export your data** (Profile → "Export my data"). Confirm it downloads
  / shares a file. (You can delete your account afterward if you want — also
  in Profile.)
- Fill out **BETA_FEEDBACK_FORM.md** (we'll send it as a form/doc).

## How to report problems (please be blunt)

For anything weird, note:
- **What you did** (the screen + the tap).
- **What you expected.**
- **What happened instead.**
- A screenshot if you can.

Especially flag:
- Anything that felt like **busywork** or pointless typing.
- Anywhere you got **stuck or confused** about what to do next.
- Anything that was **slow** or looked broken.
- The big one: **would this actually help you on race day, or is it just
  data entry?**

Don't soften it. "This part was annoying and I'd never do it" is the most
useful sentence you can give us.
